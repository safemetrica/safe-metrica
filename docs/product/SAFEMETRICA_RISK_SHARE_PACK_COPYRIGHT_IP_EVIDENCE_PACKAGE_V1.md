# SafeMetrica Risk Share Pack Copyright & IP Evidence Package v1

## 1. Purpose

This document organizes the evidence package for SafeMetrica Risk Share Pack copyright and IP preparation.

This document is not a legal opinion, patent claim, trademark filing document, or final rights analysis.

It is an internal product evidence package that summarizes:

- product structure
- workflow originality candidates
- software/process evidence
- document evidence
- screenshot candidates
- PR and commit evidence
- prohibited sensitive materials

## 2. Product Summary

Risk Share Pack is a managed commercial MVP that supports the operating record flow after a company's risk assessment has been prepared.

Core flow:

1. risk assessment sharing target prepared
2. worker share confirmation
3. worker report, near miss, or improvement suggestion
4. worker representative confirmation
5. manager review
6. Supabase ledger storage
7. customer-facing CSV export
8. monthly summary
9. next risk assessment review candidate

Risk Share Pack is not a legal-compliance guarantee, penalty-prevention service, no-accident guarantee, risk assessment outsourcing service, or safety management agency service.

## 3. Core Originality Candidate

The core originality candidate is not a simple QR form.

The core structure is the operating loop that connects:

- company-specific QR entry
- worker share confirmation with minimum identity information
- anonymous worker report option
- worker representative confirmation
- manager review
- customer-facing export with identity privacy separation
- monthly summary
- future company risk ledger reflection

## 5. Workflow Evidence

### 5.1 Worker Share Confirmation

Evidence to capture:

- QR link includes company code
- worker confirms shared risk items
- worker confirms safety measure awareness
- minimum identity information is required
- submission is stored as identified
- customer CSV displays identity fields

### 5.2 Anonymous Worker Report

Evidence to capture:

- worker can submit risk report anonymously
- identity fields are not collected or displayed
- Supabase stores identityMode as anonymous
- customer CSV keeps identity fields blank
- manager inbox shows anonymous label

### 5.3 Worker Representative Confirmation

Evidence to capture:

- linkId-based representative confirmation
- representative name, department, role, scope
- objection or follow-up opinion
- review status
- exportable confirmation record

### 5.4 Manager Review

Evidence to capture:

- manager can view share confirmations
- manager can view worker reports
- manager can distinguish identity modes
- manager can review operational candidates
- share confirmations are not treated as action-completed KPI

### 5.5 Customer Export

Evidence to capture:

- separate worker share confirmations export
- separate worker reports export
- worker representative confirmation export
- identity privacy separation
- legacy unidentified record labeling
- no raw_payload exposure in customer-facing export

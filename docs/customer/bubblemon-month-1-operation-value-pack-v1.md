# Bubblemon Month 1 Safety Operation Pack v1

## Purpose

- Show Bubblemon clear first-month operational value from SafeMetrica.
- Convert QR participation, risk-share confirmation, TBM records, and manager follow-up into customer-facing operation material.
- Keep the scope as an operation record package, not a legal judgment or compliance guarantee.

## Current operation status

- Full SafeMetrica operation.
- Notion back office is still used.
- Worker QR participation is active.
- Risk assessment candidate items have been prepared.
- Risk-share confirmation flow is being used.
- TBM is used, but still passive and mostly form-based.

## Customer-facing deliverables

1. Month 1 operation summary
2. Risk assessment implementation notice draft
3. Safety and health policy / goal draft
4. Worker participation and risk-share confirmation notice
5. Next-month action plan

## Month 1 operation summary

Include the following first-month operation record items:

- Worker QR participation count
- Risk-share confirmation count
- Field opinion / issue count
- TBM record count
- Review-needed items
- Completed or archived items
- Manager follow-up items
- Next-month focus items

## Risk assessment implementation notice draft

Include the following notice fields and placeholders:

- Announcement period
- Announcement location
- Implementation period
- Participants
- Manager/supervisor role
- Worker participation route
- Risk-share confirmation route
- Company and representative seal placeholder

## Safety and health policy / goal draft

Use cautious operation wording:

- Strengthen worker safety and health protection.
- Operate risk assessment and worker participation records.
- Share key risk factors with workers.
- Maintain TBM and monthly safety operation records.
- Review and improve identified issues.

Do not use:

- zero accident guarantee
- legal immunity
- penalty prevention guarantee
- KOSHA recognition guarantee
- AI legal judgment

## Worker participation notice

Include the following worker-facing guides and clarification:

- QR participation guide
- Risk-share confirmation guide
- Anonymous opinion / issue reporting guide
- TBM participation guide
- Notice that manager review and follow-up are recorded separately

## Next-month action plan

- Increase TBM submission habit.
- Confirm risk-share participation by department/team.
- Review worker opinions and near-miss candidates.
- Prepare improvement-before/after evidence where needed.
- Prepare monthly report for CEO/manager review.

## Internal-only notes

- Owner Console, Notion DB, Supabase, API routes, tokens, service role keys, environment variables, customer raw data, and internal IDs must not be shared with the customer.
- This package is a delivery/customer communication material.
- It does not replace final legal judgment, safety action confirmation, or employer/manager responsibility.
- It does not replace risk assessment outsourcing, official legal review, or the employer's final safety management decisions.
